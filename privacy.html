Privacy policy

This website is hosted on-premise on European servers compliant with GDPR.

To improve the content of my blog, I used website analytics. Analytics are captured using Matomo, “a powerful web analytics platform that gives you and your business 100% data ownership and user privacy protection.” The analytics engine for this website is hosted on-premise on the same servers that host the blog content and is fully GDPR compliant.

All information I collect is fully anonymised and I do not have access to any personally identifiable information (PII). Being able to see how many readers enjoy which articles is of great help to me. However, if you wish to fully opt-out from data collection on this website, you are free to do so.
